What the Flame/Comet + Two Clusters Actually Means
At short delay (15–18) you see the instantaneous glottal oscillator (the triangle = vocal
 fold snap-shut cycle).
At longer delay (300) you are now seeing the same oscillator across time:

The two distinct clusters (blue = older history, yellow = recent) are almost certainly
 two different dynamical states the larynx visited during the recording.

Most likely candidates:

Voiced speech vs. pause/breath
Two different vowel classes (different formant loading changes the effective dynamics)
Stressed vs. unstressed syllables
Speaking vs. silent rehearsal (if there was any)

The flame/comet shape is the trajectory the system takes when transitioning between 
those states. This is prosody and phonation-state geometry appearing in the 3D Takens manifold.
